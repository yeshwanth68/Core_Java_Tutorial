Final Key Word:
--------------

The final keyword in Java is a non-access modifier that can be applied to classes, methods, and variables. 
Its primary purpose is to restrict the modification of a class, method, or variable once it is assigned or declared.


1.final with variable
2.final with class
3.final with methods
4.final with parameters


final variables: Cannot be reassigned after initialization.
final methods: Cannot be overridden by subclasses.
final classes: Cannot be extended by any other class.
final parameters: Cannot be modified within the method body
